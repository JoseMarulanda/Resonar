Welcome to Resonar

Code some noises using the Freesound database with a simple syntax.

Select the sounds that you like and make sequences with them, you can look for any sound avaliable in the freesound database.

Feature

Sample

Chose your sounds with the sample function, the first argument is the sound that you want and the second is the one that you choose from the freesound search page and click the search button.

Secuence

After choosing your sounds you can sequence them in the editor, by typing if ([0,4,8,12].indexOf(idx)>=0) and in the next line player.get('kick').start(); the numbers in the array are the place where you put the sounds in a sequence of 16 steps and inside the parenthesis of the player.get() you put the sound that you searched like the following image.

Play

After writing your sequence hit the play buttom and you can start playing with the sequence you can change the numbers in real time and comment lines muting some sounds and also you can change the tempo, you have to be careful to change something other than the numbers if you want to change something more stop make your chnages and play again.
