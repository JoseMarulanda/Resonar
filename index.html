Welcome to Resonar

Code music using sounds and noises from the Freesound database with a simple syntax.

Select the sounds that you like and make sequences with them, you can look for any sound avaliable in the freesound database.

Feature

Sample

Chose your sounds with the sample function. The first argument is the sound that you choose and the second is the number that appears in the list from the freesound search page. Then click the search button.

Sequence

After choosing your sounds you can sequence them in the editor, by typing the following: if ([0,4,8,12].indexOf(idx)>=0). In the next line type: player.get('kick').start(); The numbers in the array are the place where you put the sounds in a sequence of 16 steps. Using the names of the sounds that you just wrote in the search area, type them inside of the parenthesis, as an argument of the: player.get() See the image below.

Play

After writing your sequences , click the play buttom to start playing the sequence. You can change the numbers in real time, you can make line comments to mute sounds and you can also change the tempo. You have to be careful not to change something other than the numbers, if you want to change something more click: stop, make your changes and click: play again.

About
